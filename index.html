Packing Suggestions

European River Cruise Essentials

Clothing Essentials

Comfortable walking shoes (broken in!)
Layers for changing weather (Oct in Central Europe)
Waterproof jacket or rain poncho
Smart casual for dinner (no formal required)
Warm sweater or fleece for deck time
Comfortable pants for hiking/walking tours

Activity Gear

Sturdy hiking boots (for castle hikes)
Small backpack or daypack
Water bottle (refillable)
Sunglasses and sun hat
Camera with extra batteries/memory cards
Portable phone charger/power bank

Personal Care

European power adapters (Type C & F)
Prescription medications (in original bottles)
Sunscreen and lip balm
Basic first aid items
Hand sanitizer and tissues
Travel-size toiletries (ship provides basics)

Travel Documents

Passport (valid 6+ months)
Travel insurance documents
AmaWaterways cruise documents
Flight confirmations
Emergency contact information
Copies of all documents (separate from originals)

Special Occasion

Festive outfit for Oktoberfest celebration
Comfortable shoes for dancing
Small gift/memento for birthday celebrations
Extra space in luggage for souvenirs
